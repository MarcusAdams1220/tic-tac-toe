How The Game Works:

  • This version of Tic Tac Toe includes a basketball shot clock.
  • If the shot clock expires, the game is over - So think quickly!
  • You'll have 5 seconds to place your marker before the clock expires.
  • Once your marker is placed, the clock will reset for the next player.
